Title: Dissection with the Fewest Pieces is Hard, Even to Approximate
Abstract: We prove that it is NP-hard to dissect one simple orthogonal polygon into
another using a given number of pieces, as is approximating the fewest pieces
to within a factor of $1+1/1080-\varepsilon$.
